# Privacy Policy for Body.io

**Effective Date:** January 2, 2026

Body.io (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Body.io mobile application (the “App”).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information
When you create an account or use Body.io, we may collect:
- Name and email address
- Height, weight, age, sex, and other fitness-related inputs
- Body composition metrics such as body fat percentage and muscle percentage
- Subscription status and purchase information (handled by Google Play and Apple App Store)

### b. Photos and Camera Access
Body.io may request access to your device’s **camera and photo library** so you can:
- Take or upload body photos for fitness and body-composition analysis

Photos are used **only within the app** for analysis and progress tracking. They are not shared publicly.

### c. Usage Data
We may collect limited usage information, such as:
- App interactions and feature usage
- Crash reports and performance diagnostics

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve Body.io features
- Analyze body composition and fitness trends
- Store and display your progress over time
- Manage subscriptions and premium features
- Communicate important updates or support messages
- Maintain security and prevent misuse

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:
- Secure backend infrastructure
- Restricted access to sensitive data
- Industry-standard security practices

However, no electronic storage or transmission method is 100% secure.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We **do not sell your personal data**.

Your information may be shared only with:
- Service providers required to operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
- Subscription providers (Google Play, Apple App Store)
- Legal authorities if required by law

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to provide the service or comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

---

## 6. Your Choices and Rights

You may:
- Update or edit your profile information within the app
- Revoke camera or photo permissions via your device settings
- Request account or data deletion by contacting us

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Body.io is **not intended for children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

---

## 8. Third-Party Services

Body.io may integrate with third-party services, including:
- Google Play Billing
- Apple App Store
- RevenueCat (subscription management)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
